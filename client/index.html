About

GameXRogue is a demo game on the GXCs own blockchain network. The game is design to demonstrate how the multiplayer online role- playing game(MORPG) can adopt GXCs token ecosystem into the game play. Please note that all the tokens and coins illustrated here are the examples and will not be subject to have any actual values. Please enjoy it and contact us for your opinions https://www.gamexcoin.io
